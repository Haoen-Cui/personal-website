During undergraduate years, he pursued three degrees consisting of four majors, two minors, and various certificates:

    - Departmental Honors: Highest Distinction in Mathematics and Highest Distinction in Statistics 
- Bachelor of Science in __Finance__ (Affiliated with Investment Management Academy)
    - Institutional Honors: Highest Honors 
- Bachelor of Science in __Accountancy__ 
    - Institutional Honors: Highest Honors 
- Minor in __Computer Science__ (CS) 
- Minor in __Computational Science and Engineering__ (CSE) 
- Margolis __Market Information Lab__ Certificates (All Tracks Completed: Basic, Capital Markets Trading, Investment Banking, Risk and Analytics, and Wealth Management) 
- Digital Maker Certificate for __3D Printing__
